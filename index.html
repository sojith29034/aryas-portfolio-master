About Me

I am a 17-year-old studying in 12th standard in Nowrosjee Wadia college, Pune. I completed my 10th standard from St. Arnold’s Central School, Pune. I am currently pursuing science. But wait, here’s a catch, I am also studying law. At times I feel alike. I have to study both these completely different fields at the same time. Both of them consisting of various subjects and topics. It is indeed a tough job but here comes in my balancing power. Apart from good academics I have always had interest and passion about arts. I am a self-trained artist. I mostly sketch, craft and come up with a lot of creative ideas which make me have a unique eye towards the art world. I also passed the national drawing exams with good grades. Also, I don’t lose hold on my another and major passion - Dance. I am a kathak dancer as well as a choreographer trained for 9 years. Thus a YouTuber too. Lastly, I am also a good writer.
