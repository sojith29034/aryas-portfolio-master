The Dancing Girl

Feet getting ignite
Music spread its vibe
Ghungroo got its sound
Oh look how she turns around

Her hands up so high
Trynna touch the sky
The chinking of her bangles
Oh look how she spangles

Elegance tries to embrace
The mudras that add to her grace
Her feet have increased the pace
Oh look how she kills the stage

Expressions expound the core of music
She's her own definition of classic
Her eyes follow her hands
Oh look the way she lands

Movement create moment
For audience to seek enjoyment
She dances as she lives
Oh look the way she leaves
